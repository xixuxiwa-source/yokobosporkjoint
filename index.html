The Yakobos Experience

Where flavor meets fire

Kampala's Premier Pork Destination

At Yakobos Pork Joint, we've mastered the art of fire-grilled pork. Our secret lies in the perfect blend of traditional smoking techniques and premium Ugandan pork cuts, slow-roasted to perfection over an open flame.

Located at Steka House, Near Old Kira Road, we've become the go-to spot for pork lovers seeking an authentic, flavorful experience. When the sun sets, our grill lights up, creating a vibrant night food vibe that captures the spirit of Kampala.

Every dish tells a story of passion, flavor, and local love. Join us for an unforgettable pork experience that will keep you coming back for more.
